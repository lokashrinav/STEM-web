Hackathons
Hackathons are a fun way to learn to code while coding alongside other coders to complete your goal! Certain Hackathons can help you in the competitive job market if you don't have experience! And some can help you to get recruited by a company! Hackathons are also a great way to think of ideas for a future tech startup! I would definitely recommend trying it out if you have time!

The following tiles list some hackathons and organizations that offer you could join!
DevPost
DevPost is a virtual platform that offers tens to hundreds of various hackathons hosted by different companies. These hackathons can range from developing a complex AI application to a simple mobile app. DevPost gets credibility as the "home of hackathons" and has the ability to prove it.

Click on this link if you want to go to DevPost's site: https://devpost.com/
LabLab.ai
LabLab.ai is a startup that mainly hosts fun AI hackathons. Their AI hackathons will give you a chance to build AI application projects with the latest AI tools and technology. Make sure to keep a lookout for other startups that host hackathons on their site.

Click on this link if you want to go to LabLab.ai's site: https://lablab.ai/event/
HackerEarth
HackerEarth is an online coding platform where you can take place in coding contests, data science competitions, and hackathons. It's trusted by recruiters from over a thousand companies. HackerEarth is a great way to sharpen your coding skills and have fun at the same time!

Click on this link if you want to go to HackerEarth's site: https://www.hackerearth.com/
